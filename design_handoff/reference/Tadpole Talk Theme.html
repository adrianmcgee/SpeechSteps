Brand essence

A tadpole is the start of becoming

Every choice — colour, type, the mascot, the spacing — leans toward gentle momentum. The feeling is a calm pond on a bright morning: encouraging, unhurried, and quietly proud of every little win.

Warm & encouraging

We celebrate effort, not scores. Rounded forms, soft shadows and a friendly face make practice feel like play.

Calm, never clinical

Plenty of breathing room, quiet colour, and one clear action per screen. Trustworthy enough for an SLP, gentle enough for daily home use.

Small steps, real growth

Little-and-often beats long and hard. Progress is shown kindly — a streak, a star, a word coming along.

App icon

One friendly tadpole, clear at any size

A single rounded character on a soft pond gradient. Bold shapes and a high-contrast eye keep it legible from the App Store down to a 60-point Spotlight result. No fine detail to disappear when scaled.

Meet the tadpole

A round head with a big, bright eye, a gentle smile and a flowing tail — early growth, mid-swim. The warm accent bubble ties the mascot to the in-app reward colour.

Typography

Rounded, generous, easy to read

Natively the app uses SF Pro Rounded. On the web and for these specs it maps to Baloo 2 for warm rounded headings and the big practice word, paired with Nunito — a highly legible humanist sans — for everything else. Both stay clear at large Dynamic Type sizes.
