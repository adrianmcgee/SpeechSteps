Practising at home, made calm and confident.

Speech Steps helps families support a child with Childhood Apraxia of Speech (CAS) between weekly therapy sessions — with short, parent-led practice, quick sound and sign references, and progress you can share with your speech pathologist.

Parent-led practice Sound & sign library Progress & reports Gentle reminders Private by design

Built around how CAS practice actually works

Research is clear that a few minutes, several times a day beats one long block, and that successful repetitions — not sheer volume — build the speech movements. Speech Steps keeps sessions short, celebrates the wins, and organises words by sound shape so practice follows the same progression your therapist uses.

A support, not a substitute

Speech Steps is designed to be used alongside therapy directed by a qualified speech pathologist. It does not diagnose, assess, or replace professional care. Always check new targets and techniques with your therapist.

Private by design

Everything you enter — your child's profile, targets, and practice history — stays on your device. No account, no tracking, nothing uploaded. You can read the full privacy policy.

Speech Steps is an independent app made by a parent. “Cued Articulation” and “Key Word Sign” are the names of established programs owned by their respective creators; Speech Steps is not affiliated with or endorsed by them and ships only its own original explanatory content.
